Rebel Forces

By Unblocked Games FreezeNova

Play Rebel Forces, a cool unblocked shooter game where you get to be a rebel soldier battling it out with authorities. Gear up with awesome weapons and armor, and get ready for some serious action right in your browser! Play on Unblocked Games FreezeNova now!

Controls

  • Move: Use WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Shoot: Click the left mouse button
  • Aim: Click the right mouse button
  • Reload: Press R
  • Switch Weapons: Scroll the mouse wheel
  • Jump: Press Spacebar
  • Crouch: Press C
  • Pick Up Weapon: Press F
  • Pause Menu: Press Tab

How to Play Rebel Forces

Start the game and pick a mode: campaign or Online PVP. If you choose multiplayer battle (PvP) you can enter one of the existing rooms or create your own. You can choose one of the several exciting maps. Each map has its own layout and hiding spots, so take a moment to familiarize yourself with your buildings, obstacles etc.

Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to move around the map. Staying mobile is key to avoiding being an easy target for enemies.

Press the Spacebar to jump, C key to crouch which can help you dodge enemy fire or reach different levels of the terrain.

Find strategic spots for cover, like behind walls or inside buildings, to protect yourself while reloading (press R) or planning your next move.

To shoot, click the left mouse button. Aim by clicking the right mouse button. This is essential for increasing your accuracy and getting more kills.

You can switch between weapons using the mouse wheel. Different situations might call for different weapons, so choose wisely based on your distance from the enemy or the type of cover they are using.

Weapons and Armors

As you play more and complete missions, you’ll earn in-game money. You can use them to buy better weapons and armor. From shotguns and Deagles to SMGs and even flamethrowers, each weapon has its advantages.

Armor is crucial for reducing damage from enemy attacks. Upgrading your armor can be the difference between surviving a firefight and being taken down quickly.

The main goal in each match depends on the game mode: FFA, Teams or Doomsday. But in general try to achieve the highest number of kills. Use everything you’ve learned: move strategically, aim accurately, and utilize the best weapons and armor you can afford.

Game Features

  • Weapons and Armor: Start with basic gear and get more powerful weapons and armor as you play more. Some cool weapons include the Deagle, Minigun, and Sniper. Wear armor like the Watchdog and Hellbound to protect yourself.
  • Campaign Missions: Try different missions where you have to win fights, take out enemies, or stay safe for as long as you can.
  • Daily Rewards: Come back every day to get free cash and XP points to help you level up.
  • Online PvP Mode: Play against other people online. You can set up your own game room or join one and change settings like speed and armor boosts.
